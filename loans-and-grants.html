Presidential Palliative

Application, Verification and Disbursement App

The Federal Government of Nigeria through the Ministry of Trade and Investments launched a conditional, loan and grant scheme for small businesses to grow. This platform allows the public to apply for loans and grants, the platform also allows admins(selected from the ministry) to verify applicants and pass the names to banks where the banks also do their own verification within the platform. Disbursements and repayments of loans is tracked on the platform and the various admins can track progress using the analyics page.

LMS is built with HTML on the frontend. The styling was done with Bootstrap library and a bit of pure CSS. The backend was done with Laravel. LMS is built by Zealight innovation labs.
