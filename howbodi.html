Howbodi App

Howbodi app is a telemedicine app used for Online health screening in a very fast reliable and easy way. Its one of the quickest and easiest ways to determine whether you are experiencing symptoms of a mental health condition. Mental health conditions, such as depression or anxiety, are real, common and treatable. And recovery is possible. This app provides the platform for patients to take tests, see their test score, intepretation of test results and book a medical practioner for consultation

This app is a third party app built by Zealight innovation labs. I was the frontend lead during this project and our tasks include building the user interface of this app. I was also actively involved in the API fetching process. The UI built using React/Redux and Chakra UI as the CSS library.
